Welcome!

Welcome to my academic homepage! I did my Ph. D. from Banaras Hindu University (BHU) under the supervision of Prof. Amit Pathak, focusing on **theoretical spectroscopy and molecular modeling**. This page highlights my research, publications, and professional journey.

My work focuses on **theoretical spectroscopy and molecular modeling**. I use advanced quantum chemical methods to study the electronic and vibrational properties of molecules, predict their spectroscopic signatures, and model molecular interactions. Broadly, I am interested in understanding molecular structure-property relationships and applying computational tools to various chemical and physical phenomena.

Research Interests

  • Quantum Chemical Calculations
  • Vibrational and Electronic Spectroscopy (IR, Raman, UV-Vis)
  • Molecular Dynamics Simulations
  • Computational Materials Science
  • Drug-Molecule Interactions and Sensing
